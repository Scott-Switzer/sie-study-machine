Additional Requirements
  • •No firm sponsorship needed — anyone 18+ can register
  • •Valid government-issued ID required at the test center
  • •SIE results are valid for 4 years to pair with a top-off exam
  • •Register through FINRA's TESS system (self-enrollment) or via a sponsoring firm
